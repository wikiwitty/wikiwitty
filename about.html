About

Wikipedia is full of funny and interesting articles. While reading one of them (Mariko Aoki phenomena, also my first blog post) so many jokes and GIFs were popping up in my head that I decided I should share them with other people also.

After writing a couple of blogs, I asked myself "Why not trying with YouTube videos?". So I also created an YouTube channel and started to post videos there.

I'm currently creating 60 seconds long videos on different subjects (programming, economy etc).
